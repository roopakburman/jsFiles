Tutorial

Goal of the game

Your goal is to save the good air and let them pass through and reach the bottom.

Each good air particle saved will fetch you a point.

Do not tap the good air particle or you'll loose the game!

Bad Air - A menance

Your objective is to kill bad air particles and keep them away from reaching the bottom of the screen.

Tap the bad air particle to kill them and send them away into the oblivion!

Do not tap the good air aprticle or you'll loose the game!

Friends & Foes

Carbon Monoxide is not your friend. When there is a CO particle on the screen it would make your life a living hell. Everything would become hazy and it would be very difficult to see what's going on. Tap on the CO particle to make everuthing visible!
Cup-cake is definitely your friend! When you tap a cup-cake, everything on the screen grows in size, making it easier to spot and tap the not-so-friendly angry emojis. Like everything in life, this lasts only for a few seconds but is really a great help.
Timer will pause the game for 5 seconds, giving you enogh time to kill all angry emojis on the screen. However, you loose 3 points from your score if you avail the services of a Timer.
The Ninja will clear everything on the screen, this is really usefull when there are a lot of angry emojis on your screen. However, using a Ninja Emoji will cost you 5 points from your score.
